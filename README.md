## Best Source Photos

Good inputs usually have:

- One clear face or subject outline.
- Front-facing or slightly angled posture.
- Enough shoulder and upper-body area for a believable uniform swap.
- Simple lighting without heavy blur.
- Clearly separated people for couple, team, or group shots.

Dark crops, heavy blur, or several tiny faces give the model less room to preserve identity while adding the vintage MLB look.
