## Best Source Photos

Good inputs usually have:

- A clear face or subject outline.
- Front-facing or slightly angled posture.
- Enough shoulder and upper-body area for a uniform swap.
- Simple lighting without heavy blur.
- One main subject for profile material, or clearly separated people for couple and team shots.

Photos that are too dark, heavily cropped, or full of tiny faces can still work, but they give the model less room to preserve identity while adding the vintage MLB look.
